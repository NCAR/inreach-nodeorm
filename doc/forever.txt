To use forever as a daemon process controller/monitor,
put something like this in crontab (lines split for readability):

  NODE_ENV=production

  @reboot /usr/local/bin/forever start
  -m 10 --append
  -l /usr/local/inreach-portal-nodeorm/log/forever.log
  -o /usr/local/inreach-portal-nodeorm/log/inreach-portal.out
  -e /usr/local/inreach-portal-nodeorm/log/inreach-portal.err
  --sourceDir /usr/local/inreach-portal-nodeorm
  --workingDir /usr/local/inreach-portal-nodeorm
  --watchDirectory /usr/local/inreach-portal-nodeorm
  --watchIgnore 'log/**'
  --watchIgnore '.*'
  --watchIgnore 'doc/**'
  --plain --no-colors
  bin/www

Other important environment variables are shown below
with their defaults:
  TZ=UTC
  INREACH_PORT=7997
